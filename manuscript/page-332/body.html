Be strong, my brave heart,  
And ask not "Why?"
#### Chapter II

The day of my life!  
The sun sinks,  
And the calm flood  
Already is gilded.  
Warm breathes the rock:  
Did happiness at noonday  
Take its siesta well upon it?  
In green light  
Happiness still glimmers up from the brown abyss  

Day of my life!  
Eventide's nigh,
